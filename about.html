My Hobbies

Arduino

I find tinkering with Arduino and Raspberry Pi to be incredibly fun. These platforms offer a unique opportunity to be creative, solve problems, and learn hands-on. With Arduino, I can create and program electronic circuits to control sensors, motors, and other components. And with Raspberry Pi, I can build full-fledged computer systems and automate everyday tasks.

I love the feeling of building something from scratch and seeing it come to life. There's something incredibly satisfying about troubleshooting and problem-solving to get a project working just right. And with endless possibilities, there's always something new to explore and learn.

Additionally, I love being part of the online community of makers and enthusiasts who share their ideas and projects. It's easy to find tutorials, guides, and inspiration, which makes learning and getting started a lot of fun.
